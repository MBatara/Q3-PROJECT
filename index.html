Ryan Gosling

About

Ryan Gosling (born Ryan Thomas Gosling) is a Canadian Actor born on November 12, 1980
in London, Ontario, Canada. He is well known for playing "Ken" on his latest flick, Barbie (2023)
and other movies like The Notebook (2004), Lars and the Real Girl (2007), La La Land (2016),
and Blade Runner 2049 (2017). He has 2 children with his partner Eva Mendes,
an American actress.

Early Career

Ryan Gosling started his career in 1993 when he was 13 years old. His first appearance is in
The All New Mickey Mouse Club (1993-95) and later appeared on Goosebumps (1996) and
Young Hercules (1998-99). He eventually gained popularity after starring in the
romantic drama The Notebook (2004) alongside Rachel McAdams.
